# PRODUCT.md — CEA Landscaping & Maintenance

**Register:** brand (marketing landing page; design IS the product).

## What it is
A single-page marketing site for **CEA Landscaping & Maintenance**, a woman-owned
landscaping company serving Greater Houston. It sells custom installations,
commercial maintenance, irrigation/drainage/backflow, and tree care/rockscaping
to property managers, HOAs, developers, and residential clients.

## Audience
Property decision-makers (property managers, HOA boards, developers) who need a
reliable, compliant, commercial-grade partner — plus residential clients seeking
a premium install. They scan for trust signals (licensed, insured, certified,
woman-owned) and proof of work (real project photos).

## Brand voice
Three physical-object words: **rooted, exacting, calm.**
Not loud or playful. Confident, specific, low-key premium — like a well-run firm
that lets the work speak. Copy is concrete (what we literally do), never
buzzword-marketing.

## Goal / primary action
Drive **"Request a Quote"** form submissions and phone calls. One primary CTA
throughout; everything supports getting to the quote form.

## Hard constraints
- **The hero section is locked and must not change** (video, parallax, headline
  choreography, navbar interplay). Everything else is being redesigned.
- **Real project photos appear ONLY in the projects gallery.** Every other image
  (service cards, section backgrounds, feature tiles) is AI-generated and themed.
- Reviews are placeholder samples — swap for real ones before launch.
- Quote form server action currently only logs — wire to email/CRM before launch.
